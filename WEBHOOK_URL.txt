# 🔗 GHL Webhook Configuration

## Your Railway Webhook URL (COPY THIS):
https://ghl-bridge-production.up.railway.app/webhook/new-lead

## Health Check URL (to verify server is running):
https://ghl-bridge-production.up.railway.app/

---

## How to Configure in GoHighLevel:

1. Go to GHL → Settings → Integrations → Webhooks
2. Click "Add Webhook"
3. Configuration:
   - Name: "OAC Audit Automation"
   - Trigger Event: "Form Submitted" (or "Contact Created")
   - Webhook URL: https://ghl-bridge-production.up.railway.app/webhook/new-lead
   - Method: POST
   - Content-Type: application/json

4. Map these form fields:
   - email
   - first_name
   - last_name
   - business_name
   - city
   - business_type
   - website_url
   - has_website

5. Save and test with a form submission!

---
